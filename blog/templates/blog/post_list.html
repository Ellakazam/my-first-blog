Richter City Roller Derby (RCRD) is Wellington’s all female, flat track roller derby league. Established in 2007 by a handful of girls with a vision, Richter City introduced the growing sport of Roller Derby to the country’s capital. We are a non profit, skater owned and operated league. In 2013 Richter City became a fully qualified member of the international organization governing the sport of women’s flat track derby, the Women’s Flat Track Derby Association (WFTDA).
